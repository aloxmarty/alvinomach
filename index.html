Perpetual Calendar

Used python to develop a perpetual calender algorithm that takes in user input of: day, month, year. The program prints the day of the week that the date occurred, the day of the year that date occurred and when thanksgiving was in the given year

Weather Search

Created a webpage that reports on real-time weather information from thousands of cities with the Open Weather API.

Used tkinter, time and requests libraries and deployed to Replit

News Search

Created a website that reports on real-time top news stories in the United States with the News.org API.

Used tkinter and requests libraries and deployed to Replit

More about me!

Outside of coding and the classroom, I have multiple interests. I am a massive football(the real one) fan,
music lover, avid interior design enthusiast, aviation geek, international affairs researcher and a sucker for a good book!
Here are some of my interests in greater detail

  • Aviation

    I have been enamoured by planes since I was a little boy, watching them cruise through the sky was such a marvel.
    Linked in this section are the a330neo open-source project demo, my favourite aircraft and the open-source project that began my computer science journey.

  • Renewable Energy research

    As a child, I witnessed first-hand the destruction of climate change through the flooding of my school. Electricity generation is a major greenhouse energy generator.
    I carried out research to investigate the future of renewable energy generation on the African continent and how this can be scaled up to increase power grid connection to the 660 million African's that don’t have access to it.

  • Interior Design boards

    Interior design has long been a passion of mine. I believe your colour, texture and pattern choices can really make the difference and create a space you'd love to come home to.
    Your design choices in turn speak volumes about who you are and how you see the world. Linked here is my Pinterest design board, a little window into how I’d design my idyllic space.

  • Music

    Music is the purest form of expression. Happiness, love, sadness even anticipation are all feelings that can be conveyed through song. My music palate is diverse and ranges from Afrobeat, hip-hop, British rap (drill), Rnb to indie music to name a few.
    Linked is one of my Spotify playlists that has been curated over a period of three years, it encapsulates the changes to my music taste and the types of songs that help me "chill".

  • Manchester United Football Club

    It is no secret I am a die-hard fan of Manchester United. What started as a little boy watching a team in the 2007/08 season has morphed into an undying love for a team.
    Linked in this section is a short video about "The United Way" and what it means to be a fan of the greatest football club in the world

  • Narconomics

    This peculiar book is an interesting piece by Economist Tom Wainwright as he discusses how cartels are run similarly to Multi-national Corporations.
    While this book raises numerous eyebrows, it has been one of the best books I have read as it forced me to alter the perspective through which I view things only then can you truly understand them.
